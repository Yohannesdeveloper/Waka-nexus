Executive Summary
Overview

WAKA Global Art & Music Nexus is an international cultural platform initiated from the United Arab Emirates. It is structured to provide curated exhibition opportunities and professionally governed art competitions across multiple global cities. The initiative bridges artists, institutions, and audiences across continents through structured exhibitions, professional governance, and international partnerships.

Vision & Mission
Vision: To establish a globally respected cultural institution that connects artists, cities, and creative economies through sustainable and professionally governed platforms.
  • Promote emerging and established artists on the international stage
  • Facilitate international cultural exchange and dialogue
  • Develop transparent competition standards and evaluation criteria
  • Build long-term institutional partnerships worldwide
  • Support creative economy growth in host regions
Core Activities
  • International Exhibitions: Curated visual art showcases hosted across selected global cities, featuring works from diverse artistic disciplines
  • Art Competition: Transparent evaluation process guided by an independent jury framework with published criteria and documented scoring
  • Cultural Dialogue: Panel discussions and professional networking events fostering global exchange between artists, curators, and institutions
Governance Framework

WAKA operates under a structured governance model designed to ensure transparency, fairness, and professional integrity:

  • Independent jury system with published evaluation criteria
  • Documented scoring process and conflict-of-interest policy
  • Geographic diversity representation in jury composition
  • Respect for intellectual property rights
  • Non-discriminatory selection process
  • Cultural sensitivity compliance
  • Financial transparency commitment

Investment & Growth

WAKA is structured as a scalable international cultural platform with phased expansion across major cities. The growth model includes:

  • Multi-city expansion strategy
  • Sponsorship-led revenue generation
  • Strategic institutional collaboration
  • Recurring annual editions

Use of capital includes exhibition production, operational infrastructure, governance framework development, marketing & outreach, and international coordination.
